About Me

Hi, I am Zachary Chronister. I am currently a senior at Portland State University in their Computer Science program, and I am on target to graduate in the Fall of 2022. Previously, I was enlisted in the United States Coast Guard. During the 5 years that I was enlisted, I spent 2 years entirely at sea. I served as an electronics technician with a primary focus on communications equipment. In my free time I enjoy playing video games which has inspired me to attempt to create games of my own. In the future I hope to create games that can bring others the same joy that the games I play bring me.
